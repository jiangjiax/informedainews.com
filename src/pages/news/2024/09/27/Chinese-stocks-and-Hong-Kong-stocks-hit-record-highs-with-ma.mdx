The Shanghai Composite Index approached 3,100 points, with trading volume exceeding one trillion yuan for three consecutive days. The Hang Seng Index in Hong Kong hit a new all-time high, with year-to-date gains surpassing the S&P 500. Sectors such as pharmaceuticals, securities firms, and real estate saw explosive growth, while government bond futures fell across the board. Market sentiment is optimistic, but investment remains cautious.
