Bluesky, a decentralized social network, attracted 2 million users after Brazil banned X. This surge propelled Bluesky to the top in Brazil, surpassing Instagram Threads. Globally, downloads increased by 10,584%, and in Brazil, by 1,018,952%. Bluesky's popularity expanded to 22 countries, notably growing in Portugal, Chile, Argentina, Colombia, and Romania. User engagement metrics such as likes, follows, and reposts also saw a significant increase. Bluesky's daily active users doubled, reaching 2.4 million and still rising.
