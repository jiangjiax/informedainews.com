The National Standardization Administration has issued a notice to formulate a mandatory standard, "Sanitary Requirements for Bulk Transport of Edible Vegetable Oils." This standard is led by the National Food and Reserves Administration, with participation from Henan University of Technology, Nanjing University of Finance and Economics, and the National Food and Materials Reserve Scientific Research Institute in drafting. The aim is to regulate sanitary conditions during the transportation of edible oils to ensure food safety.
