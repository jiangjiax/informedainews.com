Microsoft plans to celebrate the 25th anniversary of Xbox and Halo. John Friend, Head of Xbox Consumer Products, revealed that the company is preparing related activities. Since its launch in 2001, Xbox has expanded from a gaming console to Windows PC and mobile gaming, becoming one of the largest gaming communities in the world. The Halo series, as Xbox's flagship product, has achieved over $1.8 billion in commercial success in merchandise and licensing since its launch in 2001. Microsoft's recent acquisitions of Activision-Blizzard and Bethesda have further expanded its game lineup. Specific celebration plans have not yet been announced.
