The company calculates operating costs using the moving weighted average method. Revenue from the "iMoutai" platform was 10.25 billion yuan, a growth of 9.76%; revenue from series wines was 13.147 billion yuan, an increase of 30.51%. Over the next three years, the company will pay cash dividends of no less than 75% of the net profit attributable to shareholders annually, with at least two dividend payments each year.

The moving weighted average method is a cost calculation method that dynamically adjusts the average cost based on the purchase cost and quantity of inventory.
