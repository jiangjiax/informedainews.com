Shanghai plans to establish a 10 billion yuan Future Industry Fund with a duration of 15 years. The fund will adopt a model combining sub-funds and direct investments, focusing on supporting disruptive innovation, multidisciplinary cross-innovation, and frontier technology platforms. The fund will operate in a market-oriented manner, adopting a tolerant attitude towards failure and implementing due diligence exemptions.

**Explanation:**
- **Disruptive Innovation:** Refers to innovations that can fundamentally change existing markets or industries.
- **Multidisciplinary Cross-Innovation:** Refers to innovations that combine knowledge from multiple disciplines.
- **Frontier Technology Platform:** Refers to technology platforms at the forefront of scientific and technological development.

Shanghai has recently established multiple industry funds with a total scale of 100 billion yuan, focusing on supporting areas such as artificial intelligence and intelligent manufacturing.
