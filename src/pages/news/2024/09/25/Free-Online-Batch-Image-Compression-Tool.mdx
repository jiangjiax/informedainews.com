Compress JPG is a free online tool for batch image compression. It uses Google's Squoosh algorithm and supports JPG, PNG, WebP, and more.

Key features:
- Compress up to 1000 images at once.
- No file size limits.
- 100% privacy; files stay on your device.
- No registration needed.

Visit: https://compressjpg.io
