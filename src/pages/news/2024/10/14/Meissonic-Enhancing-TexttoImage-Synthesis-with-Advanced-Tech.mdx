Meissonic enhances text-to-image synthesis by integrating non-autoregressive masked modeling with cutting-edge methods. It outperforms current models such as SDXL in both quality and resolution. Key advancements include optimized sampling, positional encoding, and feature compression. Meissonic produces 1024x1024 images, establishing a new benchmark.

*Autoregressive models: Generate data sequentially, token by token.
*Non-autoregressive models: Generate data in parallel, faster but less sequential.*
