Sun Yingsha becomes the first table tennis player to break the 10,000-point mark, reaching 10,800 points. She secured 2,000 points by winning the women's singles title at the WTT China Grand Slam, surpassing teammate Wang Manyu. Wang Manyu ranks second with 7,450 points, and Chinese players occupy the top four spots in the women's singles.

19-year-old Lin Shidong ranks third in the world, setting a new career high with 4,965 points. He defeated Ma Long in the men's singles final of the WTT China Grand Slam. Chinese players occupy the top five spots in the men's singles.

The WTT China Grand Slam has boosted the rankings of several Chinese players, with Fan Siqi and He Zhuojia rising to 35th and 41st, respectively. Xiang Peng and Danish player Anders Lind have also significantly improved their rankings.
