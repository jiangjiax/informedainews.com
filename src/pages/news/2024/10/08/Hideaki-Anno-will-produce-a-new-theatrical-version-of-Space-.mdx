Hideaki Anno announces that Khara has obtained the license to produce a new "Space Battleship Yamato" theatrical film. The original series premiered in 1974, telling the story of the Earth Defense Force's adventure in transforming the Yamato battleship. The new project is set to launch in 2025, differing from the remakes "Space Battleship Yamato 2199" and "Eternal Yamato REBEL3199." Anno expresses that if not for encountering this work 50 years ago, he would not be who he is today. He will create a work that blends the old and the new with respect and gratitude.
