Lamucal.com offers a free tool for converting YouTube videos into guitar tabs, chord charts, and main melodies. It also removes vocals and generates AI-driven covers, expediting the process of music creation.

Guitar tabs are diagrams that indicate which strings to play and where to place fingers. Chord charts depict finger positions for specific chords.

This tool simplifies music transcription, benefiting musicians at all skill levels.
