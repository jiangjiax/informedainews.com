Memowise is a browser plugin designed to help users save and organize online content efficiently. By allowing users to highlight text, add tags, and take notes, it ensures that important information is easily retrievable. These notes are synchronized across devices for convenient review and search capabilities. Additionally, an AI chat feature is integrated to assist users in making the most of their saved notes. Memowise also integrates with WeChat Reading and organizes notes from social media, enhancing its utility for users who engage with a variety of digital content.
