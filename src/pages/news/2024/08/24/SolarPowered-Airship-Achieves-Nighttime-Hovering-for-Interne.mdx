Sceye's HAPS, a 65-meter solar-powered helium airship, hovers in the stratosphere. It provides internet access to remote areas, remaining airborne for months. First flown in 2021, it charges during the day and hovers at night. It is slated for a commercial launch in 2025.

HAPS stands for High-Altitude Platform Station, a drone-like device that operates at high altitudes, typically in the stratosphere.
