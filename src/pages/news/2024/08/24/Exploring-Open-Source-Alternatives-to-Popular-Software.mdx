In "17 Open Source Tools That Could Replace Your Favorite Software," Madza explores alternatives to popular software. These open-source tools offer cost savings, flexibility, and customization.

1. **Drawio** replaces LucidChart. It's free, supports offline use, and integrates with Google Drive and GitHub.
2. **Penpot** is an open-source Figma alternative. It supports real-time collaboration and cross-platform compatibility.
3. **Hoppscotch** is a lightweight API development tool, faster and simpler than Postman.
4. **Supertokens** offers open-source authentication, simplifying user management and enhancing security.
5. **n8n** automates workflows, connecting various apps and services without vendor lock-in.

These tools enhance productivity and offer customizable solutions. They reflect a growing trend towards open-source software, providing viable alternatives to proprietary systems.
