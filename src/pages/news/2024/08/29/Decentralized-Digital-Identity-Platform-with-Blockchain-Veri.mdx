Dentity is a digital identity platform that utilizes open standards and is fully compliant with W3C guidelines. Users have full control over their data and identity. Dentity partners with ENS to verify identities on the blockchain.

Adopting open identity standards ensures the creation of interoperable and secure digital identity systems. Compliance with W3C standards guarantees compatibility and security across the web. ENS, or the Ethereum Name Service, is a decentralized naming system for the Ethereum blockchain, facilitating the association of identities with blockchain addresses.
