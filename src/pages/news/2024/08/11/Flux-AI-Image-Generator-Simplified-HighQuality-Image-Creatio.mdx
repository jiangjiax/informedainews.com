Flux AI Image Generator creates high-quality images. It employs two AI models, Flux.1 pro and Flux.1 schenell, with five resolution options. Users input keywords; AI refines prompts for better visuals.

A prompt market helps craft effective image descriptions, lowering entry barriers. Free trials are available, with paid options for enhanced features.

Visit flux-ai.io to experience the technology.

**Explanation of Terms:**
- **AI Models:** Software programs designed to perform specific tasks by analyzing data and learning from it.
- **Resolution Options:** Different levels of image clarity and detail.
- **Prompt Market:** A platform where users can find or share descriptions to guide the AI in image creation.
