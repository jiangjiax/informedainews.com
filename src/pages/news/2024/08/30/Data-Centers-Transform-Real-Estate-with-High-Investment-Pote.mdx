Data centers, key to internet and AI, are booming. The U.S. leads with over 2,800, followed by the UK. Land values skyrocket, tenfold in some cases. Demand surges with digitalization. Private equity firms, like Blackstone, eye data centers as top investments. Land near power grids fetches higher prices. Data centers value proximity to power lines. This sector transforms real estate, driving up land values near critical infrastructure.
