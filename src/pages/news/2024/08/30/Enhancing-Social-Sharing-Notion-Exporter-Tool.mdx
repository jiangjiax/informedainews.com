Notion Exporter is a browser extension for Chrome and Edge. It converts Notion pages into scrollable images or cards, maintaining the original layout and styles. This tool simplifies sharing on social media by integrating Notion's writing and management features.

Insight: Notion Exporter enhances Notion's functionality, making content sharing more straightforward while preserving its sophisticated formatting.

Explanation:
- **Notion**: A widely-used productivity and note-taking application.
- **Browser extension**: A small software component that customizes web browser capabilities.
