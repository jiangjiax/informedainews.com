Renowned leaker billbil-kun reveals details about the PS5 Pro.

1. The official name is PS5 Pro.
2. The design is similar to the PS5 Slim, with a white main body, two USB-C ports, a power button, and three black stripes on the side panel, possibly related to replaceable side panels. Slightly thicker than the PS5 Slim.
3. The sketch does not show an optical drive, its existence or need for separate purchase is not clarified.
4. The controller remains the DualSense, with no new changes.
5. Expected to be announced in early September 2024.

**Explanation**:
- **USB-C interface**: A hardware interface standard for Universal Serial Bus (USB).
- **DualSense controller**: The standard controller for Sony's PlayStation 5 gaming console.
