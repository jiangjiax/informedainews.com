The criminals utilize artificial intelligence to alter faces and voices, making detection difficult.

The impact of this issue is widespread, affecting over 200 schools and attracting thousands of participants in Telegram chat rooms. Authorities have responded by increasing penalties for such crimes and establishing special task forces.
