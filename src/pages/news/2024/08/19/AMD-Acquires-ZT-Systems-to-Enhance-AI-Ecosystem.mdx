AMD's significant acquisition: purchasing ZT Systems for $4.9 billion. This transaction combines cash and stock, with an additional potential $400 million contingent on achieving specified targets. ZT Systems, headquartered in New Jersey, is renowned for its expertise in data center infrastructure, collaborating with industry leaders such as Nvidia and Intel.

The objective? To enhance AMD's AI ecosystem. ZT contributes significant knowledge in AI system design, encompassing both hardware and software. This bolsters AMD's capability to market more chips and systems to its clients. Dr. Lisa Su, AMD's CEO, views this move as pivotal in their AI strategy, focusing on providing efficient, scalable solutions for cloud and enterprise clients.

Frank Zhang, CEO of ZT Systems, will lead AMD's manufacturing division. The acquisition is expected to finalize in the first half of 2025.
