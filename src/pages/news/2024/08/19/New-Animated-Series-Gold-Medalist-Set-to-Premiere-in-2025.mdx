"Gold Medalist," a figure skating manga illustrated by Kata Tsubota, began serialization in Monthly Afternoon in 2020. It won first place in the "Next Manga Awards 2022" and the General Audience Category of the "68th Shogakukan Manga Award 2023."

The anime adaptation has been announced, with a new trailer released and set to premiere in January 2025. Produced by ENGI, directed by Yasuhiro Yamamoto, scripted by Jukki Hanada, and character designs by Chika Kameyama.

The story centers on a young man whose dreams have been shattered and a girl who has been abandoned, who meet on the ice and form a partnership, aiming for the world stage.
