Google has introduced new anti-theft features for Android 15 in Brazil, including automatic and remote locking. These features utilize device sensors and artificial intelligence to detect unusual behaviors such as rapid movement or abnormal network activity. Remote locking and data wiping require an internet connection, enhancing security. Brazil, known for its significant issue with cell phone theft, is an ideal testing ground. The new measures restrict custom ROM installation, reducing the likelihood of stolen phones being resold.
