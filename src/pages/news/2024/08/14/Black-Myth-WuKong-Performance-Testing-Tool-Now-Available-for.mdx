Users can customize settings and preview performance under different quality settings through real-time rendering scenes. The test data helps the development team optimize game quality.

Actual tests show that the combination of i5-12600KF and 4060Ti achieves an average frame rate of 66 frames per second at 2K resolution. The Steam Deck achieves an average frame rate of 59 frames per second at low quality settings.
