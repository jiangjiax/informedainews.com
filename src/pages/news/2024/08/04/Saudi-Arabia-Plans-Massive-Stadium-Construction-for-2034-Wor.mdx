Saudi Arabia plans to build 11 new stadiums for the 2034 World Cup, with an estimated total investment potentially reaching hundreds of billions of dollars. The event will be held in five cities, including Riyadh and Jeddah. The new stadiums will be equipped with the latest technology to ensure an excellent experience for fans. Saudi Arabia also plans to provide over 230,000 hotel rooms. This initiative is part of Saudi Arabia's "Vision 2030," aimed at reducing dependence on oil and promoting economic diversification.
