Didi Special Car has introduced a pet-friendly service, covering 49 cities. Users can call through the app or mini-program, select the pet option, and enjoy "pet-friendly no refusal to carry." There are three types of charges: 3.9 yuan for bringing your own pet carrier, 14.9 yuan for medium-sized pets without a carrier, and 22.9 yuan for large pets without a carrier. New users receive coupons, up to 20 yuan off. Drivers voluntarily sign up, undergo professional training, and the car is equipped with pet urine pads and disinfectants.
