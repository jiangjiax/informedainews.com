Shenzhen has deployed 170 outdoor smart gyms across parks and communities, serving over 5.85 million citizens with a cumulative usage of 50 million times. These facilities integrate AI, the Internet of Things, cloud computing, and big data technologies, offering physical fitness tests and exercise guidance. They feature testing areas, digital training zones, and competition zones, supporting diverse workouts and competitions.

The smart gyms are strategically located in high-traffic large parks such as Shenzhen Bay and Lotus Mountain Park to ensure high utilization rates. The integration of technology enhances the citizens' exercise experience and promotes a healthy lifestyle.
