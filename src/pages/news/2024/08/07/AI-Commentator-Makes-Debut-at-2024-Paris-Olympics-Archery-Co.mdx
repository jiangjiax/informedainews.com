SenseTime partners with SMT to introduce an AI commentator for the archery competition at the 2024 Paris Olympics. Utilizing 3D motion capture and AR effects, the technology tracks the trajectory of arrows to enhance broadcast visualization. This technology is also used in table tennis event broadcasts. SMT, a leading domestic media technology service provider, has served multiple global sports events.

**Explanation:**
- **3D Motion Capture:** Technology that captures the movement of objects in three-dimensional space.
- **AR Effects Rendering:** Augmented reality technology that adds virtual elements to real scenes.
