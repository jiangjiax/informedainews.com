Yang Xiao Chu, Tang Zhongqiu's Chinese restaurant in Paris, became a hotspot during the Olympics. Located near the table tennis arena, it attracted Olympians, fans, and journalists. Tang, originally from Xiamen, China, opened the restaurant in France two decades ago.

The restaurant, known for its authentic Sichuan dishes, saw a surge in customers. Athletes like Ding Ning and Zhang Yining, both Olympic gold medalists, frequented the place. Tang handled the crowds calmly, ensuring quality over speed.

Chinese fans, traveling worldwide to support their players, filled the restaurant daily. The atmosphere was lively, with cheers echoing as diners watched matches on a large TV.

Tang's restaurant became a home base for many, offering a taste of home amidst the French cuisine. The success of Yang Xiao Chu during the Olympics highlighted the universal appeal of good food and community.
