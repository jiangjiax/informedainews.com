At the 2024 Asian Tech Conference in Singapore, China's technological advancements were prominently featured, with Chongqing's Ma Shang Consumer Finance Co., Ltd. (Ma Shang) taking center stage. This digital financial institution presented its innovative "Sky Mirror" financial model, designed to enhance security and user satisfaction in digital banking.

Ma Shang's model, acclaimed for its sophistication and effectiveness, successfully mitigates 99.9% of potential security threats and increases user satisfaction by 28%. The model's success highlights China's burgeoning strength in financial technology, positioning it as a frontrunner in digital finance.

Furthermore, Ma Shang unveiled the "Fu Hui Yang" smart farming platform, which utilizes AI to decrease farming costs and boost sales, demonstrating technology's pivotal role in rural development.

Chongqing, historically not a tech hub, is evolving into a digital financial center through initiatives such as the "Intelligence Finance and Smooth Flow" project. This transformation underscores the city's strategic shift towards becoming a major player in global digital finance.

Ma Shang's dedication to innovation and international collaboration, evident through its global talent recruitment and technological advancements, positions it as a reliable global financial services provider.

**Key Terms Explained:**
- **Digital Financial Institution:** A company that offers financial services through digital technologies, including online banking, mobile payments, and digital currencies.
- **AI (Artificial Intelligence):** Technology that empowers machines to execute tasks usually requiring human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.
- **PCT Patent:** A patent application filed under the Patent Cooperation Treaty, enabling applicants to seek invention protection across multiple countries simultaneously.
