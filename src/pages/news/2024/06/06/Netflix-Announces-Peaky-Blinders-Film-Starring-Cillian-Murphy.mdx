Netflix announces the film adaptation of "Peaky Blinders," starring Cillian Murphy, with filming set to commence in September. Murphy, expressing his joy at reuniting with creators Steven Knight and Tom Harper, views the project as a gift to fans.

"Peaky Blinders" is a British series centered around the Shelby criminal family in post-WWI Birmingham. The title refers to the gang's practice of sewing razor blades into the peaks of their caps.

Murphy's character, Tommy Shelby, the gang's strategic and complex leader, has enthralled audiences. The move to film promises a deeper exploration of this iconic figure and his world.
