**Summary:**
《Elden Ring: The Shadow of the Golden Tree》PS5 version, with a capacity of 16.502GB, is scheduled for pre-load on June 19th and will be officially released on June 21st. The game's base version has been updated to 01.012.000, with a total capacity of 54.230GB. The new DLC includes over a hundred weapons, a dozen main bosses, and mini-bosses.

**Explanation:**
- **Pre-load:** Allows players to download the game files before the official release, so they can start playing immediately upon launch.
- **DLC (Downloadable Content):** Additional content provided after the game's release, typically adding new missions, characters, or items.
- **BOSS:** A particularly powerful enemy in the game, often marking the end of a chapter or a significant turning point.

**Insights:**
《Elden Ring》is renowned for its profound worldview and challenging combat. The new DLC,《The Shadow of the Golden Tree》, not only expands the game's content but also deepens the player's experience. The introduction of over a hundred new weapons and a variety of boss designs suggests an increase in game difficulty and exploration fun. For players who love challenges, this is undoubtedly an update worth anticipating.
