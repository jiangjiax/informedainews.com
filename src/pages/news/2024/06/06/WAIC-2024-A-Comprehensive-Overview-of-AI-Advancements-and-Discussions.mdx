The World Artificial Intelligence Conference (WAIC 2024) is scheduled to take place in Shanghai from July 4 to 7. This international event, themed "Collaborative Consultation for Shared Benefits, Good Governance for Smart Solutions," will feature a comprehensive agenda including forums, exhibitions, awards, and immersive experiences. More than 500 companies will occupy 52,000 square meters of exhibition space, displaying the latest AI technologies. Prominent figures, including recipients of prestigious awards such as the Turing, Marr, and Fields medals, will explore key AI topics including ethics, large models, and data. Beyond the conference, various competitions and smart applications will be showcased, aiming to promote year-round innovation and industrial integration.

**Key Terms Explained:**
- **Turing, Marr, and Fields Medals:** These are international accolades that recognize significant contributions in computer science, cognitive science, and mathematics, respectively.
- **AI Ethics:** The examination of what is morally right and wrong, good and bad, concerning the design, development, deployment, and use of artificial intelligence systems.
- **Large Models:** Sophisticated AI models that necessitate significant computational resources and data for training, commonly used for complex tasks such as natural language processing and image recognition.
- **Data:** In the context of AI, data refers to the information used to train AI models, encompassing text, images, audio, and other forms of digital content.
