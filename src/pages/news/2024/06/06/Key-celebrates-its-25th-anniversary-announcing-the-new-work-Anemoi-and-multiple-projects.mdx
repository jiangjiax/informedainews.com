Key社庆祝25周年，开设纪念网站，公布多项企划。网站推出特别映像，开设官方Discord，Switch游戏打折，《Kanon》Steam版即将上架，售卖纪念周边。6月14日，直播活动将举行，丘野塔也、魁等将出席，公布新作《anemoi》及更多周年企划详情。

Key社, a renowned Japanese game company known for its emotionally resonant visual novel games, is celebrating its 25th anniversary. To commemorate this milestone, they have launched a memorial website featuring various projects. The site includes a special video, the opening of an official Discord server, discounts on Switch games, the upcoming release of "Kanon" on Steam, and the sale of commemorative merchandise. On June 14th, a live streaming event will take place, where notable figures like Tatsuya Okano and Kai will attend. They will unveil a new game titled "anemoi" and provide further details on the anniversary projects.
