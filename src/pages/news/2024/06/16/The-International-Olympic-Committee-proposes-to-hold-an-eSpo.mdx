The International Olympic Committee proposes to host the Esports Olympic Games, scheduled for 2025 or 2026. This proposal is based on Singapore's "Olympic Esports Week," which combines sports simulation with electronic gaming to attract a younger audience. The Esports Olympic Games will adopt a new structure, departing from traditional models.

Esports Olympic Games: A novel competition that integrates traditional esports with sports simulation, aiming to extend the influence of the Olympic movement into the digital realm.
