Summary:
Prototype announces the release of Key社's classic game, "Kanon," on Steam, with support for English, Japanese, and Simplified Chinese. The Switch version will also update to include these languages. Notably, the Steam edition features full voice acting and touch controls compatible with tablets and Steam Deck. Originally launched in 1999, "Kanon" has sold over 300,000 copies and inspired an anime adaptation. Following Visual Art's acquisition by Tencent in 2023, Key社 now operates under the Chinese conglomerate, though Tencent assures creative autonomy for the studio.
