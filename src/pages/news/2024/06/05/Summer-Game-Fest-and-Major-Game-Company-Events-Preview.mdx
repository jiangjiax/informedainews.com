The gaming world shifts focus from E3's demise to the Summer Game Fest, hosted by Geoff Keighley, and a series of high-profile game company events. This June, major players like Microsoft, Sony, and EA will unveil new titles and updates. Notably, 2K plans to reveal a major sequel at the festival.

Key events include:
- **Summer Game Fest** on June 8, featuring announcements from top developers and a special segment, "逛丢一只鞋," recorded live.
- **Devolver Digital's** celebration of their mascot Volvy's 15th birthday, promising quirky reveals and giveaways.
- **Xbox's** special event on June 10, where details on upcoming games like "Call of Duty: Black Ops 6" and potential news on a new "Gears of War" title will be disclosed.
- **Ubisoft's** showcase on June 11, highlighting new games like "Star Wars: Outlaws" and updates on ongoing titles, with exclusive deals for viewers.

These events, streamed live on Bilibili via Gcores' official channel, promise a month of gaming excitement, with each event tailored to excite and engage the gaming community.
