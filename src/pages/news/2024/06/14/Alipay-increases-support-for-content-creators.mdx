**Summary:**
Alipay announces a commitment of 1 billion in cash and 10 billion in traffic to support content creators. Since its opening, the monthly active creator base has grown tenfold. The plan includes launching a new Creator Center, a Financial Intelligence Channel, and introducing advertising resources, enhancing short video and live-streaming commerce capabilities to facilitate commercialization.

**Insight:**
This move signifies Alipay's strategic expansion into the content domain, leveraging both financial and traffic support to accelerate the commercialization of the creator ecosystem. It also reflects the growing emphasis and investment trends of digital platforms in the content economy.
