LunaAI.Video transforms text and images into high-quality videos using AI. It's designed for both professionals and hobbyists, offering diverse input options and real-time access. Free daily usage is available.

Dream Machine, part of Luma AI, utilizes Transformer models trained on videos, ensuring efficiency and regular updates.

Transformer models are AI architectures that process data in parallel, enhancing the speed and quality of machine learning tasks, particularly in natural language processing and now, video processing.
