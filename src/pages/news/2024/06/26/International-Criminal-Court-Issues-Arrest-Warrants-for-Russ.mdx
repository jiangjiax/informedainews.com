The International Criminal Court has issued arrest warrants for Russian officials, including former Defense Minister Sergei Shoigu and Chief of Staff Gen. Valery Gerasimov, in connection with attacks on Ukrainian civilian infrastructure. They are accused of war crimes and inhumane acts, specifically targeting electric infrastructure between October 2022 and March 2023. Additionally, a warrant has been issued for President Putin regarding child abductions. This legal action highlights a global judicial response to alleged atrocities, signaling a significant advancement in holding military leaders and heads of state accountable.
