Novo Nordisk, the manufacturer of Ozempic and Wegovy, is planning a $4.1 billion expansion in Clayton, NC, which will create 1,000 jobs. The new 1.4 million square-foot facility, slated for completion by 2029, will double their capacity in the Raleigh-Durham area, focusing on treatments for weight loss and diabetes. This marks North Carolina's largest investment in the life sciences sector, with the new jobs offering an average salary of $70,000. This expansion not only bolsters the local economy but also highlights the increasing demand for effective medical solutions in weight management and diabetes care.
