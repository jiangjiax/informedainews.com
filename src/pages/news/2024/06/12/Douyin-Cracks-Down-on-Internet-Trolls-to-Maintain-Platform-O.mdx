Summary:
Since May 2024, Douyin has intensified its crackdown on internet trolls, removing over ten thousand non-compliant videos and penalizing more than 1.33 million accounts. The focus has been on combating rumor-mongering and privacy invasion, utilizing technological means to identify and address non-compliant content.

Insight:
Internet trolls, akin to a spreading plague, erode authenticity and trust. Douyin's actions, though belated, are necessary. To purify the online environment, sustained effort and wisdom must go hand in hand.
