Telegram, boasting 400 million users, now integrates bots and mini apps to facilitate the sale of digital goods and services. Introducing Telegram Stars, a currency specifically designed for digital purchases such as e-books, courses, and games.

Telegram Stars can be acquired through in-app purchases on Apple and Google platforms or via PremiumBot, in compliance with app store policies. Developers have the option to convert these Stars into Toncoins, a cryptocurrency, using Fragment.

Transactions involving physical products continue to use Telegram's existing payment systems without alteration. This update significantly broadens business opportunities within Telegram, potentially reaching up to 900 million users.

Future enhancements will include gifting features for content creators, marking Telegram's transition into a more comprehensive digital marketplace.
