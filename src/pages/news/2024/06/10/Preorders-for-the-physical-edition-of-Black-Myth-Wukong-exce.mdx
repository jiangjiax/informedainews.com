The physical deluxe edition is priced at 820 yuan, including collectibles and a game activation code; the collector's edition is priced at 1998 yuan, featuring a protagonist figurine. The game is based on "Journey to the West," integrating Chinese culture, and is hailed as a beacon of domestic AAA games.
