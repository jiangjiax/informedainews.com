Artifact, the news app created by Instagram founders, now drives Yahoo News. Accessible on both iOS and Android platforms, it customizes news according to user interests, provides summaries for extensive articles, and allows content filtering based on preferences. Yahoo's acquisition of Artifact in April has integrated its AI to improve news delivery. The updated app maintains certain social sharing features and introduces a game-like system of reading badges.
