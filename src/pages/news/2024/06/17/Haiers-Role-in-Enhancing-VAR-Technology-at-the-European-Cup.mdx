The European Cup, a quadrennial spectacle, captivated fans worldwide despite late kick-offs. Amidst the excitement, VAR (Video Assistant Referee), a technology aiding referees in critical decisions, emerged as a focal point. Utilizing multiple cameras for precise video replays, VAR assists in crucial calls such as penalties and red cards, enhancing fairness but occasionally disrupting the game's flow.

This year, Chinese brand Haier, renowned for its technological prowess in television manufacturing, partnered with VAR as the official display provider. Haier's involvement goes beyond advertising; their high-tech displays are crucial to VAR operations, ensuring clear, precise visuals for referees. This partnership not only highlights Haier's global reach but also underscores their commitment to innovation in sports technology.

As the tournament progresses, Haier's presence in VAR rooms and on-field displays will likely enhance their brand visibility, potentially accelerating their transition from a prominent global player to a market leader.
