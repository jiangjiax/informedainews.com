Despite initial skepticism, CUDA now supports a vast developer community, solidifying Nvidia's hold on AI computing.

The company's rapid growth—from $1 trillion to $3 trillion in market cap in under a year—underscores its strategic importance in the AI revolution. Nvidia's chips power advancements across industries, from autonomous vehicles to natural language processing. The transition from a graphics-focused company to an AI powerhouse reflects a bold pivot, driven by visionary leadership and technological innovation.
