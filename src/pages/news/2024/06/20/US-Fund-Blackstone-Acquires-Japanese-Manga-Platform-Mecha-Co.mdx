Blackstone, a US fund, is acquiring Infocom, the operator of Mecha Comic, a prominent Japanese online manga platform. This $1.7 billion deal is designed to leverage the growing global manga market. Since its launch in 2006, Mecha Comic has expanded its market presence, including a subsidiary in the US. The Japanese manga industry, highly valued by the government, reached an all-time high in 2023. Blackstone intends to further develop and animate popular titles.
