**Summary:**
WeChat Keyboard has been updated to version 1.2.0.585 for Windows, introducing an AI assistant feature. After entering content, users can press the "=" key to receive a response generated by AI. This update also includes recommendations for emojis and kaomoji, optimizes the input format for dates and times, and hints at the possibility of similar features being rolled out on Android and iOS platforms in the future.
