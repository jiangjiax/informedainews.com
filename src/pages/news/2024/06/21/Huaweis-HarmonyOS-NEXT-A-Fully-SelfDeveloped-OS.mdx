Huawei's Yu Chengdong announced the fully self-developed HarmonyOS, built on OpenHarmony. This Chinese-origin system challenges the duopoly in mobile OS, offering a secure alternative. It's a milestone, marking a decade's leap equivalent to three in Western tech.

Key milestones:
- HarmonyOS NEXT launched for developer beta testing.
- In-house "Cangjie" programming language unveiled.
- Seven devices upgraded to HarmonyOS 4.2.
- Sales of "HarmonyOS Smart Car" hit 134,000 units.
- Huawei's ADS smart driving logged over 220 million km.
- HarmonyOS NEXT developer version debuts in June.
- Over 4,000 native apps for HarmonyOS NEXT.
- New M7 Max model to feature advanced 192-line laser radar.
- Major upgrades slated for flagship products post-September.
