BrainyAI, a Chrome extension, brings AI to your browsing. It integrates models like GPT-4 and Claude, allowing chat, search, and document interaction from a sidebar. Free, it competes with Sider AI, Monica, and Merlin.

Features:
- Multi-model conversations.
- Multiple AI search engines.
- Web and YouTube summaries.
- Document AI interaction.

GPT-4 and Claude are advanced AI models capable of complex interactions, enhancing user experience without additional cost.
