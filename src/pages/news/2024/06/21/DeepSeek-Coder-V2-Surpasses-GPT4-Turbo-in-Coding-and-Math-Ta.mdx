DeepSeek Coder V2, an open-source coding model developed by Chinese AI startup DeepSeek, has surpassed GPT-4 Turbo and other leading models in coding and math tasks. This model supports over 300 programming languages and excels in complex coding challenges, outperforming both open and closed-source competitors. Enhancements in DeepSeek Coder V2 include expanded language support and a larger context window, enabling it to effectively handle extensive coding tasks. The model is available under a MIT license, facilitating both research and commercial use, and can be accessed via download or API.

This advancement highlights the rapid evolution of AI in coding and the potential for open-source models to lead innovation.
