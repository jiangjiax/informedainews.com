Apple is broadening its AI educational offerings, providing Developer Academy students and alumni with foundational AI training. Beginning in the fall, participants will acquire skills in constructing, training, and deploying machine learning models tailored for Apple devices. The curriculum, which encompasses 18 academies worldwide, delves into Core ML and AI model development. New tools, such as Swift Assist in Xcode 16, along with enhancements in Swift 6, are designed to enhance creativity and efficiency in app development.
