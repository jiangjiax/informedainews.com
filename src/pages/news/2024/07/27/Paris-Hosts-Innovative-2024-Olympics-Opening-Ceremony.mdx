Paris opened the 2024 Olympics with a river parade. Athletes from 200 nations, excluding Russians and Belarusians, sailed on the Seine. Macron declared the Games open. 329 events in 32 sports will run until August 11.

The ceremony marked a first: an outdoor, non-stadium opening. Paris, hosting again after 1900 and 1924, showcased innovation. The exclusion of Russians and Belarusians reflects ongoing geopolitical tensions.
