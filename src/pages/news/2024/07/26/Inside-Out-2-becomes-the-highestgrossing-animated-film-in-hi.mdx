"Inside Out 2" sets a new box office record, surpassing "Frozen 2" to become the highest-grossing animated film of all time. It has earned $14.62 billion globally, outpacing both "Mario" and "Frozen 2." In North America, it raked in $5.98 billion, and $8.49 billion internationally. Its opening weekend ranks fifth all-time, behind "Barbie." The data is sourced from Amazon's Box Office Mojo website.
