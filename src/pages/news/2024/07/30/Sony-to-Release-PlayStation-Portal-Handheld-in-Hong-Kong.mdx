Sony will release the cloud handheld device PlayStation Portal Hong Kong version on September 4, 2024, priced at HK$1580.
This device is a handheld accessory for the PS5, equipped with an 8-inch LCD screen and DualSense controller functions.
Through Remote Play, it can connect to the PS5 via Wi-Fi and play games already installed on the console.
The PlayStation Portal cannot run content independently and requires dependency on the PS5.
Pre-orders will open on August 5.
