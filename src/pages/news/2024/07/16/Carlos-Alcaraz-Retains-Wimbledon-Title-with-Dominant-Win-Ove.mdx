Carlos Alcaraz, 21, dominated Novak Djokovic 6-2, 6-2, 7-6 (4) to retain his Wimbledon title. The Spaniard's serve and athleticism overwhelmed Djokovic, who aimed to match Roger Federer's eight Wimbledon titles. Alcaraz's fourth grand slam win positions him as a tennis great. Djokovic struggled with double faults and Alcaraz's relentless pace, only finding form in the tight third set. Alcaraz sealed victory in a tie-break, cheered by a royal audience.
