EA Sports introduces the Ultimate Team mode for College Football 25. In this mode, players can draft a mix of current and former college athletes to build their ideal team, including NFL stars who started their careers in college football.

Ultimate Team mode is a gaming feature that allows users to collect and manage virtual trading cards of athletes to compete in simulated sports matches.
