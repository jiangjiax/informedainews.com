Tsuburaya announced that the "Ultraman Card Game" will be released on October 25, 2024.
This card set combines the worldview of "Ultraman" with gameplay, suitable for all age groups.
The cards feature newly illustrated artwork and character photos, showcasing character scenes and abilities.
Available in Japanese, English, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese, with simultaneous release in 15 countries and regions worldwide.
A world championship will be held in 2025.
A pre-release playtest event will be held in July, with Ryuusei Yokohama discussing the appeal of the cards.
