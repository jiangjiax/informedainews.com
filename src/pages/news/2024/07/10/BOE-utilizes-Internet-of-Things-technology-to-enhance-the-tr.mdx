BOE collaborates with the Chinese Fencing Team to introduce IoT technology for enhanced training efficiency. Mini LED display technology captures detailed sword movements, while AI-integrated smart devices assist in daily training. Smart health equipment monitors team members' vital signs, and waterfall screens showcase achievements and stories.

Mini LED: A display technology with extremely small pixel pitch, offering ultra-high contrast.
AI Smart Integrated Machine: A device integrating artificial intelligence functions for training and academic exchange.
Waterfall Screen: A display composed of multiple LCD panels, supporting interaction and touch.
