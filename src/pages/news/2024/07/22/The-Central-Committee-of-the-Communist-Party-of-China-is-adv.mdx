The goal is to increase affordable housing to meet the needs of the working class and support housing improvements. Policy adjustments include relaxing housing purchase restrictions, reforming financing and pre-sale systems, and improving real estate taxation. City governments are granted autonomy in regulation, allowing for tailored policies based on local conditions.
