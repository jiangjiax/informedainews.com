Beijing Weici Technology has launched Marvel MEG, a 128-channel helium-free magnetoencephalography (MEG) scanner. Operating at room temperature without the need for liquid helium or magnetic shielding rooms, it efficiently detects neurological disorders. Fosun Pharma is responsible for its commercialization.

Magnetoencephalography (MEG) is a diagnostic device that utilizes the weak magnetic fields generated by brain electrical activity. Helium-free: The device does not rely on liquid helium for cooling, reducing maintenance costs and operational complexity.
