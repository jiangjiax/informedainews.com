The Central Committee of the Communist Party plans to legislate to promote the development of the private sector. Key aspects include:
- Removing barriers to market entry
- Enhancing financing support
- Strengthening regulation
- Supporting private enterprises in participating in major technological breakthroughs and projects
- Improving credit evaluation and credit enhancement systems
- Strengthening corporate governance and compliance construction

Additionally, efforts will be made to strengthen anti-monopoly and anti-unfair competition measures, and to eliminate regulations that hinder fair competition.

This initiative aims to boost the development of the private sector and enhance market vitality.
