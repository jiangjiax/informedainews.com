Sony has partnered with Spin Master to launch "The Shapes Collection" figurine series, featuring characters from "God of War: Ragnarok," "Horizon Forbidden West," and "Ghost of Tsushima." The figurines stand at 15.24 cm tall, boast intricate details, multiple movable joints, and interchangeable parts. The Aloy figurine is available for pre-order at $49.99, including 12 accessories. Pre-orders for other character figurines will open soon, priced at $29.99 each. The figurines are crafted based on in-game digital models to ensure precise detailing, with an expected shipping date in August.
