England's Gareth Southgate replaced defender Ezri Konsa for the suspended Marc Guéhi. No other changes were made. Switzerland's Murat Yakin maintained his lineup steady. Both teams are set to clash in the Euro 2024 quarterfinals.

- Guéhi out due to suspension.
- Bellingham remains despite potential ban.
- Switzerland unchanged after defeating Italy.

Key players:
- England: Pickford, Walker, Rice, Stones, Saka, Kane, Bellingham, Foden, Trippier, Konsa, Mainoo.
- Switzerland: Sommer, Akanji, Embolo, Freuler, Xhaka, Rodriguez, Vargas, Ndoye, Aebischer, Schär, Rieder.
