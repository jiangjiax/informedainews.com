Alipay's wealth management page has launched a "Global Investment" section, introducing QDII (Qualified Domestic Institutional Investor) funds that cover multiple markets including the United States, Japan, and Europe. This move aligns with investors' needs for global asset allocation and emphasizes the importance of balanced diversification across various assets.

QDII funds, which allow domestic investors to invest in overseas markets, have recently gained popularity due to their impressive market performance, with their managed assets reaching new highs.

Ant Fund emphasizes diversified asset allocation, not focusing solely on any particular sector, and encourages users to spread their investments for healthy financial management. Platform data shows an increase in the proportion of users practicing diversified asset allocation.

Overall, investors' interest in overseas investments has grown, but A-share related assets remain a focal point of user attention. Ant Fund continues to guide users to focus on high-quality index funds to smooth market volatility.
