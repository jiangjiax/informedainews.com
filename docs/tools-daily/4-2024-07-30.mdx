### Efficient Asynchronous Video Interview Software

Vhire automates video interviews. It reviews a thousand candidates in five minutes. New users get three free tries.

Asynchronous interviews mean candidates record answers for later review. This saves time and scales the process.

**Explanation:**
- **Asynchronous:** Not happening at the same time. Here, it means candidates and interviewers don't need to be online together.

TicketGenius, a Jira plugin, leverages AI to enhance project management. It automates ticket creation, story division, and epic linking. Available on the Atlassian Marketplace, it aims to simplify Jira usage.

Jira: A tool for managing projects.

### "Custom AI Analyst Builder for Precise Business Data Queries"

AI Analyst Builder crafts bespoke AI experts without the need for coding. It addresses business data inquiries through chat interfaces, akin to ChatGPT. This tool delivers precise answers, curtailing "hallucination"—the phenomenon of incorrect responses.

In the context of AI, hallucination refers to the system providing erroneous answers. This tool is dedicated to dependable data analysis, aiming to reduce such mistakes.

OFFLIGHT streamlines task management, calendaring, and time tracking. It integrates tasks with schedules, enhancing focus on priorities and productivity. The tool effectively monitors and analyzes the time spent on tasks.

Calendaring involves organizing and planning time and events. Time tracking measures how long tasks take. Both are crucial for balancing work and life.

### Free AI Text-to-Speech Tool with Multilingual Support

LuvVoice offers a free Text-to-Speech (TTS) tool. It supports over 70 languages and 200 AI voices. No word limit, simple interface. Users can download MP3s or listen online. Free for all.

Ideal for:
- Content creators
- Educators
- Writers
- Marketers
- Language learners

TTS converts written text into spoken audio.
