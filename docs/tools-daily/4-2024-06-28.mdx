### Weimang Plan: Efficient Task Management and OKR Goal Tool

**Microshine Plan** - A lightweight (only 5M) efficiency management tool, built on Tauri, with the website: [kestrel-task.cn](https://kestrel-task.cn).

Features include:
- **Task Management**: Conveniently add and track tasks.
- **OKR Goal Management**: Helps clarify corporate strategy and enhance team collaboration.
- **Pomodoro Technique**: 25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes of rest, optimizing work efficiency.
- **Calendar View**: Task reminders under either Gregorian or lunar calendar.
- **Quick Notes**: Instantly capture ideas and inspirations.

Clean and efficient, with low memory consumption, suitable for users pursuing efficiency.

**Explanation:**
- **OKR**: Objectives and Key Results, a goal-setting framework used to clarify and track objectives and their outcomes.
- **Pomodoro Technique**: A time management method that enhances focus and efficiency through short work and rest cycles.
