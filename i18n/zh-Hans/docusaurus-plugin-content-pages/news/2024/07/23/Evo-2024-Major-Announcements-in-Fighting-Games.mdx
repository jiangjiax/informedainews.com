Evo 2024 brought big announcements for fighting game enthusiasts. Highlights include:

- **Guilty Gear -Strive-**: Adding Lucy from Cyberpunk: Edgerunners as a guest character in its Season 4 pass, set for 2025.
- **MultiVersus**: Introducing Samurai Jack and Beetlejuice in its second season.
- **Tekken 8**: Heihachi Mishima returns, despite his apparent demise in Tekken 7.
- **2XKO**: Riot Games' fighting game revealed more details, including a free-to-play model and delayed inclusion of Katarina.
- **SNK vs. Capcom: SVC Chaos**: Unexpectedly re-released on Steam, with plans for Switch and PS4.
- **Granblue Fantasy Versus: Rising**: Adding new character Versusia and significant updates on August 20.

The event showcased top-tier competition, with memorable matches in Mortal Kombat 1, Guilty Gear -Strive-, and Street Fighter 6.
