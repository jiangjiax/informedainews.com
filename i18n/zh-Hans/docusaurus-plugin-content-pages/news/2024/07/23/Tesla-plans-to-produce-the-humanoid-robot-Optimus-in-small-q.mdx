Tesla CEO Elon Musk announced that small-scale production of the humanoid robot Optimus will begin next year for internal use. The plan is to ramp up to large-scale production by 2026 and potentially supply other companies. The second generation of Optimus has seen a 30% increase in walking speed and enhanced finger functionality, suitable for light gripping and heavy lifting. Tesla intends to first validate its practicality in factories before offering it for external sales. Musk predicts that Optimus will significantly boost the company's profits and market value, potentially reaching $25 trillion.

Humanoid robot: A robot with a form and functions similar to a human, designed to perform complex tasks.
