Google and NBCUniversal are bringing AI to the Paris Olympics. Using Google's Gemini AI, viewers will receive personalized sports highlights narrated by an AI version of sports commentator Al Michaels. The AI will also assist in real-time searches during broadcasts, helping fans understand game rules and athlete details.

Comedian Leslie Jones will use Gemini to explore Olympic events, adding humor and insights. Google Maps will offer 3D views of Olympic venues, enhancing the virtual tour experience.

This tech integration aims to modernize Olympic coverage, making it more engaging for younger audiences. It's a bold step, blending technology with traditional sports broadcasting.
