In the sweltering summer, various regions showcase their unique ice treats. Guizhou's ice pulp is made with fresh fruits, sticky rice, ice, and sugar, with the sticky rice adding a smooth texture and layers. The classic cucumber milk ice pulp is incredibly refreshing. Mango-flavored ice pulp is rich in fruit, exuding a strong tropical vibe.

Tianjin's traditional shaved ice features hawthorn, apricot, sour cake, and red beans, with a mix of sour and sweet flavors, generous on the toppings but light on the ice. Snowflake yogurt, the Tianjin equivalent of a frappuccino, is delicate and delicious.

Beijing's milk custard fruit ice combines custard with strawberry slush, offering a creamy texture with a hint of fermented aroma. Watermelon custard is a summer exclusive, made with watermelon juice.

Xinjiang's Sharanduoke is a shaved ice with yogurt, pleasantly sour and sweet. Maloshi is a milk ice cream, dense and smooth, with a rich aroma of milk and eggs.

Chaoshan's five-fruit soup allows for free combinations of ingredients like pearl barley, longan, and lotus seeds, providing a cool relief from the heat. Mung bean and kelp soup has mung beans that turn into a sandy texture, with kelp strands adding a chewiness.

Sichuan's ice jelly, ice dumplings, and ice tofu are excellent summer coolers. The three-treasure ice jelly includes white fungus, fermented rice, and small dumplings, offering a rich texture. Ice dumplings are made with crushed ice, fermented rice, and brown sugar water, perfect for alleviating spiciness. Ice-soaked tofu is simply made with fermented rice water and cool tofu, a heavenly treat.

Yunnan's Paoluoda and papaya water are street desserts. Papaya water is made with fake sour seed jelly, with rose sugar as a highlight. Paoluoda consists of bread crumbs, milk, and coconut milk, cool and sweet.

Hainan's Qingbu Liang is based on coconut water or coconut milk, with a variety of toppings. Coconut milk shaved ice Qingbu Liang features fine ice shavings with a strong coconut aroma, providing a refreshing and satisfying experience.

Each region's ice treat has its own charm, offering a cool respite in the summer heat.
