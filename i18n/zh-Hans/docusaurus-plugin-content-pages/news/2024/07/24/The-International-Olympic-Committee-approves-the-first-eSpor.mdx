The International Olympic Committee unanimously approved the hosting of the first e-Sports Olympic Games in Saudi Arabia, scheduled for 2025. The cooperation agreement spans 12 years.

The e-Sports Olympic Games, abbreviated as e-Olympics, is a new digital competition event established by the International Olympic Committee. This initiative aims to attract a younger audience and strengthen the influence of the Olympic brand in the new era.

The Saudi Olympic Committee will provide professional expertise in the field of e-sports, with the collaboration based on the Olympic Charter and values.
