SquatBrand has registered personalized domain names for all 10,466 athletes participating in the 2024 Paris Olympics. These domains, available in various suffixes such as .com and .io, are designed to enhance athletes' digital presence. Athletes have the opportunity to purchase these domains for a nominal fee, starting at $1,500 each. If not claimed, the domains will redirect to OnlyFans. This initiative provides athletes with a new global platform to showcase their brands.
