WeChat Subscription Accounts are testing a new feature called "Express News," which pushes the latest news sourced from official media, ensuring authority. Users can receive these updates even if they haven't subscribed to the account. Express News may appear in the newsfeed, and users have the option to disable it.

Gray-scale testing: A small-scale test conducted on a portion of users before a software or service is fully launched.

WeChat has also recently tested other features such as "Listen" and "WeChat Installments," demonstrating its continuous efforts to innovate and adapt to user needs.
