A team from the University of Melbourne in Australia has, for the first time, simulated biological molecular systems with quantum precision using a supercomputer. This technology can accurately predict molecular chemical reactions and physical properties, which is crucial for the development of new drugs.

Traditional drug development is time-consuming and carries high risks. The new method, through quantum mechanics and high-performance computing, offers unprecedented speed and accuracy, accelerating the process of drug discovery.

This research not only enhances the efficiency of drug development but also potentially provides new treatment options for intractable diseases.
